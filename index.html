  • Nebula

    A large, diffuse cloud of gas and dust in space where new stars are born.

  • Earth

    The third planet from the Sun, the only known celestial body to support life.

  • Orbit

    The curved path that an object, such as a planet or moon, takes around another celestial body, usually due to gravity.

  • Meteor

    A small celestial body entering the Earth's atmosphere, commonly known as a shooting star.

  • Mercury

    The smallest and closest planet to the Sun in our solar system.
